Our Philosophy

We believe a bathroom
is more than a room.

It's the first space you inhabit each morning — and the last at night. Inline Bathrooms was founded on a single conviction: that the quality of a space shapes the quality of a life. Every project we take on is managed from concept to completion with the precision, care, and discretion that premium design demands.

We work with a curated network of specialist tradespeople, material suppliers, and interior consultants. No compromises. No shortcuts. No surprises.

What We Do

A complete service,
from first brief to final finish.

01

Design Consultation

Your renovation begins with a dedicated design consultation. We listen, assess the space, and develop a bespoke concept that reflects your taste, lifestyle, and the architecture of your home.

02

Project Management

Every Inline renovation is managed in full — from scheduling and procurement to site oversight and quality control. You have a single point of contact throughout. No chasing. No confusion.

03

Curated Materials

We source tiles, fixtures, and fittings from trusted premium suppliers. From natural stone to bespoke cabinetry, each material is chosen for longevity, finish quality, and aesthetic integrity.

04

Expert Installation

Our hand-selected tradespeople are specialists in high-end bathroom installation. Every detail — from waterproofing and tiling to plumbing and lighting — is executed to exacting standards.

05

Aftercare Guarantee

Our relationship doesn't end at handover. Inline provides a comprehensive aftercare package so your bathroom continues to perform and look exceptional for years to come.

06

Renovation Refresh

Not every bathroom needs a full renovation. Our Refresh service updates key elements — fixtures, surfaces, lighting — to revitalise your space without the disruption of a complete overhaul.

How It Works

A process built around you.

01

Initial Consultation

We meet at your property for a no-obligation consultation. We assess the space, discuss your vision, and outline a realistic scope and investment for your renovation.

02

Design & Planning

Our design team develops detailed plans, material selections, and product specifications. You approve every decision before a single item is ordered or tradesperson booked.

03

Managed Build

Work begins on an agreed timeline. Your Inline project manager is on site every day. You receive regular updates and have direct access to your manager at every stage.

04

Final Handover

We complete a full quality inspection before handover. We walk you through your new bathroom, explain every fixture and finish, and leave you with a complete aftercare documentation pack.
